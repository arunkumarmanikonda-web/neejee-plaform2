# Phase 1 scope

## Objective
Stabilize auth/session, env/runtime consistency, and deploy safety without touching commerce truth layers.

## In scope
- auth and session risk scanning
- logout safety checks
- env/runtime validation
- preflight reporting
- repo guardrails
- admin/config exposure reduction

## Out of scope
- checkout and payment logic
- finance and GST
- stock truth and inventory movement
- taxonomy redesign
- pricing master logic
- warehouse logic
- inventory loading
- catalogue build
- ERP integration
